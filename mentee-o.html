What Is A Mentee?

YOU! The Cree Youth are referred to as Mentees within this mentor program. A mentee is a person who is advised and counselled by a mentor who is a Cree member or friends of the Cree. These Mentors are industry leaders with expertise in their field of work.

The concept is that a mentee will have the role of driving the relationship between you and your mentor, developing a plan, resourcing information, and making a commitment to continuous learning throughout this process.

Mentees have the benefit of different mentors within different industries in regards to gaining exposure to a variety of styles, opinions, and experiences.

Preparing self

Before a mentee seeks a mentor, a few introspective steps are necessary. First, each mentee must clarify his or her values by exploring what motivates them. Second, each mentee is required to research an industry of interest before they reach out to a mentor.
