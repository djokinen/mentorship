About Mentorship

The Cree Youth Mentor Program is a proactive and unique, online social networking tool exclusively for the Cree Youth. This program provides the opportunity to seek expertise regarding careers, support and guidance from successful and experiences mentors.

It is a relationship between a mentor and a mentee that is based on mutual trust and respect. This relationship can provide potential internships, job shadowing and possible employment that are only attainable through the connections of the mentor.

Overall, the Cree Youth Mentor Program is a space to learn and share.

Benefits of Mentoring

This mentor program is designed to benefit the mentees by providing insight and education towards becoming the person you want to be. Other benefits include:

  • help set career goals and steps to realize them
  • access to a support system during critical stages of career development
  • exposure and insight from personal experiences
  • learn specific skills and knowledge that are relevant to personal growth
  • critical feedback
