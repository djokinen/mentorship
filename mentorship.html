Mentorship

THE MENTORSHIP NETWORK IS A:

Proactive, unique, online social networking tool, for current players and Cree youth to be able to seek expertise regarding careers, support, and guidance

Tool to make connections with Mentors, Successful Cree, and friends of the Cree who have experience in various industries/careers

Proactive opportunity to register for internships, job shadowing and full time jobs that are posted by connected Mentors

Overall, it is a space to learn and share.
